Deployment of Version 1. 09. 22- Portal Browser Independence and Introducing Chatbot Support The Live Portal has been optimized for seamless functionality across multiple browsers. While it previously required specific browser settings, it now offers full compatibility with popular browsers such as Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, and Microsoft Edge. This update removes the need for browser-specific configurations, delivering a smoother and more flexible user experience. Additionally, a new Chatbot feature has been integrated into the portal to assist users by addressing frequently asked questions and resolving common queries, complementing the existing Helpdesk Support. This enhancement aims to provide faster assistance and a more efficient support experience. 2. Operational Help Book for Bidders on Preferential Bid Submission in IOCL E-Tender portal Attached Help-book contain Stepwise Instructions on Submission of Preferential Bid in IOCL e-Tender Portal. Bidders are informed to go through the Document thoroughly to correctly avail Purchase Preference in IOCL E-Tender Portal. Please note that once a bid is submitted Purchase Preference cannot be changed. Bidders are advised to submit Queries at-least 2 Working Days before the Due Date of Submission of Tender. 3. Restriction in IOCL E-Tendering Portal towards the number of users against single PAN Bidders to note that there is restriction in IOCL E-Tendering Portal towards the number of Users IDs against single PAN. Hence bidders especially corporates, companies, partnership firms etc are advised to exercise caution in creating new User IDs and are advised to use already available User IDs. Further Bidders are advised to avoid generation of multiple User IDs against single PAN. 4. Mail/SMSs alerts are in-built in the eProcurement portal as an additional feature to inform department users as well as bidders on various events that are happening in the portal. However, delivery of such mail / sms to the concerned individual will always depends on the configuration of individual account in the portal, receivers mail / sms server, mail box / mobile capacity and other factors. Hence, it is advised to visit the website/portal regularly to keep themselves updated and to act upon the changes/modification/actions deemed fit in any manner to be carried out in the tender. 5.
