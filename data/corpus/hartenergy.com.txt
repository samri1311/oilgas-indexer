(ERIK VERREPT/Adobe Stock) HE By Hart Energy Staff November 12, 2025 01: 55 AM Key Takeaways The initiative is in line with the Trump administration’s executive order on deploying advanced nuclear reactor technologies for national security. The Aurora facility is expected to fabricate fuel for Aurora-INL, which was selected for the DOE’s Reactor Pilot Program. The Department of Energy (DOE) approved the Nuclear Safety Design agreement for Oklo’s Aurora Fuel Fabrication Facility at the Idaho National Laboratory (INL), according to a Nov. 11 press release.
